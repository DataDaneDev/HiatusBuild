## Slide-install checklist (the part that matters most for non-removable setups)

Before ordering, verify these with exact model dimensions from the product page/spec PDF:

- **Overall width + handle protrusion** fits your slide tray with `>=0.5 in` side clearance.
- **Lid swing clearance** (many fridges need rear or top clearance to fully open).
- **Ventilation gap** around compressor side: target `>=1.5 in` at compressor intake/exhaust side.
- **Tie-down points** align with slide anchors (or add an adapter plate).
- **12V wiring path** supports full extension without rubbing; use strain relief near the fridge socket.
- **Low-voltage cutoff setting** can be adjusted for your house electrical strategy.
